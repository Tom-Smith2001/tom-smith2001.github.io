Intro

I am Tom Smith, a university student and aspiring game/software developer. I have been coding and developing projects for years, and this is a showcase of some of what I have created, displaying my skills and creativity. Click the image for a video if applicable.

ASGEMovieGame

'ASGEMovieGame' was a project created in the same bespoke C++ engine as ASGETrek, but was a project I worked on as part of a small team of 4. It is a local PvP multiplayer game based on Ghost In The Shell (1995), exploring the themes of espionage and hacking present in the movie by giving one player control of a hunter with a gun that must track down and kill the other hiding player amongst crouds of NPCs as they hack terminals.

ASGETrek

ASGETrek was a clone of EGATREK, a classic strategy game wherein the player takes control of a starship and has to traverse a galaxy and destroy enemies through the use of text commands. Using a bespoke C++ based engine created for my university course, I recreated it from the ground up.

Random terrain generation

Using perlin noise, I created a system in Unity to generate random terrain, with variables editable by the user such as the ground type, the amplitude and frequency of the function, and the size of the landscape created.
